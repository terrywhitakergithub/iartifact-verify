About iArtifact

iArtifact is a desktop application for sealing and verifying digital works — software, documents, media — with a cryptographic provenance chain, RFC 3161 trusted timestamps, and an optional registry entry. This page is the client-side verifier: everything runs locally in your browser. No upload. No account.
